Infi Leaf - The leaf Wetness Sensor

About Infi Leaf

Infi Leaf is an accurate leaf wetness sensor, Uses capacitive grid technology and comes in different leaf and cable variations. Infi Leaf determines Leaf wetness over time and its values are used to predict infection of several hosts by three different phytopathogenic fungi.


Features

  • Doesn't require painting or coating
  • Measurement range: 0 (dry) to 100% (wet)
  • Preconditioned
  • 3-meter cable
  • Easy Setup

Applications

  • Leaf Wetness monitoring.
