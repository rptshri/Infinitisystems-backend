Infi Drive - Smart and Powerfull Motor Driver

About Infi-Drive

Infi Drive is an Easy to use, Smart Closed loop motor driver with overvoltage, overcurrent protection, constant rpm modes, Goto position, position hold modes, and easy i2c, pwn, servo interfaces, infi Drive is one step solution for your needs.


Features

  • Works from 8V to 30V/50V(for HV model)
  • Max current capacity 60A cont 120A pulse 10% Duty
  • All modes have tunable parameters
  • Integrated Self test
  • I2c address set by Dip switch

Applications

  • Motor Driver for Industrial and robotics.
